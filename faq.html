Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our accessories. If you don't see what you're looking for, contact us.

How do I care for my accessories?
Store jewelry in a cool, dry place to prevent tarnishing. Clean bags with a soft cloth and mild soap. Avoid exposure to water or harsh chemicals.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Contact us for a return label. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.
Do you offer international shipping?
Yes, we ship worldwide. Shipping costs and times vary by location. Check our products page for estimates.
Are your products sustainable?
We prioritize eco-friendly materials like recycled metals and organic fabrics. Learn more on our About page.
How do I know my size for jewelry?
Check our sizing guide on the product page. If unsure, measure your finger or wrist and compare to our chart.
Can I customize accessories?
Yes, we offer custom engraving on select items. Email us with details for a quote.
